Our Team

The entrepreneurs behind the vision
Michael Wylie, Chairman

Mike has been in the construction industry for more than forty years and this has allowed him to experience almost every situation imaginable. As recently retired Chairman of Wilson-Bayley Homes (WBHO), which is a blue-chip construction firm listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange with annual revenue of £1.6 billion, Mike was involved in huge property projects which required intricate knowledge and powerful management. As Chairman of Quanta Group, he brings a wealth of experience and insight.

If not immersed in business you will find him enjoying time with his grandkids, or on the golf course putting in some practice to avoid ever getting beat by Rob!
Robert Du Toit, CEO

Rob is an entrepreneur in the purest form, who came from nothing and set up his own venture from scratch. He learned his trade in South Africa trading residential property more than 15 years ago, and since then his entrepreneurial spirit has not stopped growing. Rob is the founder and architect of the Quanta Group operating system and watches with pride as the current team execute everything with passion. He continually seeks to improve and refine each area of the business, which allows further expansion to be achieved.

In his (limited!) spare time you will find him on the driving range or letting off steam in his Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) classes. Time with his family and his treasured dogs are also on the downtime list.
Kathryn Osland, Joint Head Of Operations

Kathryn finished University in 2009 with a degree in Law. She started her career in residential conveyancing department of a Law Firm shortly after graduating. Having her first taste of the property market Kathryn worked for a listed Property Asset Management company based in Exeter.

A relocation to London then secured her role with Quanta Group where she has progressed from a role in sales progression to being joint Head of Operations. Her role now involves the overseeing of the purchase and resale portfolio along with day to day running of the operation. Her favorite part of the role is the fast pace of the sales environment and the chance to continuously learn within a great team.
Shannon Eastoe, Joint Head Of Operations

Shannon joined the Quanta Group team in 2016 after securing her First Class Master's degree in law. Having worked for a number of solicitors throughout University, she wanted to work in a role with a faster pace and joined the Quanta team within days of her final exams. Shannon joined the team as a Sales Progression Assistant working alongside the administration team and has worked hard to progress throughout the company and now oversees the daily activities as joint Head of Operations.

Day to day, Shannon manages the acquisition and resale pipeline, values property and oversees the residential conveyancing for each property that is added to the portfolio. Shannon has an eye for detail, key for making efficient and sustainable property buying decisions while maximising margins on resales.

When she is not at work, Shannon is usually found pottering in the garden or on a long run.
Neil Hall, CRM Manager

Neil is a successful CRM manager with a passion for both problem solving and system development through his logical and efficiency driven thought process. With 10 years' experience in the property industry and a skillset which exceeds his CRM Manager title, Neil is able to help Quanta Group drive efficiency and innovation whilst always ensuring the data supports the decisions. Neil's ability to understand and easily explain requirements to both end users & directors alike greatly reduces the time required to implement beneficial changes in the business.

When he isn't head down in the latest development for Quanta Group he will most likely be in his studio working on his passion for dance music, or out in nature with his camera.
